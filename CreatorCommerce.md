## Creator Commerce Intelligence Layer

We are building the intelligence layer for creator-led commerce.

Modern eCommerce is shifting. Brands are no longer built only through paid ads — they are built through creators, audiences, and owned distribution. But while creators drive attention, very few teams truly understand how that attention converts into revenue.

Our platform exists at the intersection of creators and commerce operators.

### The Problem

Creator-led brands generate traffic through content, but:
- Funnel visibility is fragmented.
- Attribution is unclear.
- Revenue is not mapped cleanly to creators or content.
- Operators lack structured insight to optimize performance.
- Creators lack ownership visibility into how their influence converts.

Most teams operate on surface metrics (views, clicks, sales totals) instead of structured revenue intelligence.

### Our Position

We provide infrastructure that connects:

**Content → Traffic → Funnel Behavior → Revenue**

For operators, this means:
- Multi-store and multi-creator performance visibility
- Funnel diagnostics to identify revenue leaks
- Insight-driven optimization recommendations
- Revenue unlock hypotheses backed by data

For creators, this means:
- Clear performance dashboards
- Revenue attribution transparency
- Incentive alignment
- A sense of ownership over their business impact

### Our Focus

We are not building a generic analytics dashboard.

We are building a system that:
- Understands creator-driven traffic
- Identifies where revenue is locked
- Translates data into actionable intelligence
- Evolves through continuous signal absorption

Our long-term vision is to become the operating intelligence layer for teams building brands around creators — enabling scalable, insight-driven creator commerce.
