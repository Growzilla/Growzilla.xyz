# Growzilla Website Copy Suggestions
> Last Updated: January 2026

## Executive Summary

The current Growzilla.xyz copy is strong on energy but could better align with how customers think and search. These suggestions make the messaging more familiar (using existing mental models), reduce perceived risk, and increase specificity.

---

## Current vs. Recommended Copy Comparison

### Hero Section (Growzilla Page)

**Current:**
> "Crush Leaks. Scale BIG."
> "Our AI artifact analyzes your traffic data and stomps out conversion leaks before they become revenue sinkholes."

**Issues:**
- "AI artifact" is unclear (what is an artifact?)
- "Traffic data" is vague
- "Revenue sinkholes" - unfamiliar metaphor

**Recommended:**
> **Headline:** "Find the Leaks. Fix the Revenue."
> **Subhead:** "Your Shopify store is losing money right now. Our AI finds exactly where customers drop off—and tells you how to fix it in 90 seconds."

**Why it's better:**
- Immediate clarity on problem + solution
- Specific time promise (90 seconds)
- "Shopify store" immediately qualifies the reader
- "Drop off" is familiar customer language

---

### Subhead / Value Prop

**Current:**
> "Stop guessing why customers leave. Start growing with AI-powered insights."

**Issues:**
- "Insights" is a weak word (everyone says it)
- Doesn't differentiate from Triple Whale/others

**Recommended:**
> "Most analytics tools show you dashboards. Growzilla tells you exactly what to change."

**Why it's better:**
- Direct competitive contrast
- Action-oriented ("what to change")
- Differentiates mechanism, not just outcomes

---

### Social Proof Line

**Current:**
> "Join 200+ elite brands in the herd"

**Issues:**
- "Herd" may not resonate with everyone
- No specificity on outcomes

**Recommended:**
> "$47M+ recovered for 200+ elite brands"
> "See what [Brand Name], [Brand Name], and [Brand Name] found."

**Why it's better:**
- Leads with outcome ($47M)
- Named brands build trust
- "Recovered" implies they were losing money (creates urgency)

---

### CTA Button

**Current:**
> "Install Now" / "Join Waitlist"

**Issues:**
- "Install Now" implies commitment before seeing value
- "Waitlist" implies delay

**Recommended:**
> **Primary:** "See Your Leaks Free"
> **Secondary:** "Get Your Free Store Audit"

**Why it's better:**
- Value-first (see your leaks before committing)
- "Free" reduces risk
- "Your" makes it personal

## Section-by-Section Recommendations

### Problem Section

**Current Copy Direction:**
Generic problem about "losing customers" and "not knowing why"

**Recommended Problem Statement:**

```
THE PROBLEM

Your Shopify store has holes.

Right now, customers are visiting your site, getting
interested, and then... leaving. You don't know where.
You don't know why. You just know your conversion rate
is stuck and your CAC keeps climbing.

You've tried:
• Staring at Google Analytics (didn't help)
• Adding more dashboards (made it worse)
• Asking your agency (they blamed iOS)
• Throwing more ad spend at it (burned cash)

The problem isn't more data.
The problem is no one's telling you what to DO with it.
```

**Why it works:**
- Lists specific failed solutions (familiar pain)
- Names the real problem (action, not data)
- Creates recognition ("that's me")

---

Growzilla finds your leaks in 90 seconds.

### FAQ Section

**Recommended FAQs (customer-language focused):**

**Q: How is this different from Triple Whale?**
> Triple Whale shows you dashboards. Growzilla tells you what to change. We don't just report data—we analyze it and give you specific prescriptions. Think of it as the difference between a thermometer (Triple Whale) and a doctor (Growzilla).

**Q: Will this work with my existing tools?**
> Yes. Growzilla works alongside Klaviyo, Gorgias, and other tools in your stack. We're the brain that makes them smarter, not a replacement.

**Q: How long until I see results?**
> Most brands see their first prescriptions within 90 seconds of connecting. Meaningful revenue impact typically happens within 2-4 weeks of implementing the AI's recommendations.

**Q: Is my data safe?**
> We're GDPR-compliant and based in Sweden (Europe's strictest privacy laws). Your data is encrypted, never sold, and you can delete it anytime.

**Q: What if the AI is wrong?**
> Every prescription comes with an explanation and confidence score. You decide what to implement. And our team reviews edge cases to catch anything unusual.

## Summary: Key Copy Principles

1. **Lead with problems they already recognize**
2. **Use specific numbers everywhere possible**
3. **"Prescriptions" not "insights" (action > data)**
4. **Risk reducers in every section (free, fast, safe)**
5. **Competitor contrast without attacking**
6. **Customer language, not feature language**
7. **Short paragraphs, scannable structure**
