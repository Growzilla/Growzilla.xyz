## SECTION 3: HOW IT WORKS

### Section Header
```
How It Actually Works
```

### Subhead
```
Growzilla is an analytical layer that sits on top of your existing tools.
It connects your data, identifies conversion friction, and generates
prioritized recommendations. Here's the process.
```

### Step 1
```
STEP 1: Connect Your Existing Tools

One-click connection to Shopify. API connections to Meta, Google,
Klaviyo, and your other tools.

We don't replace your stack—we connect it.

• 90-second Shopify installation
• No developer required
• Your data stays in your tools
• We read, we don't write

What happens: Growzilla pulls order data, traffic data, ad performance,
and customer behavior into a unified data model.
```

### Step 2
```
STEP 2: Continuous Analysis Begins

Within 24 hours, Growzilla has analyzed:

• Where traffic drops off in your funnel
• Which ad campaigns actually drive profitable orders
• Which products convert vs. which attract but don't sell
• Where mobile experience differs from desktop
• Which customer segments have highest lifetime value

This runs continuously. Not once—always.
```

### Step 3
```
STEP 3: Ranked Recommendations, Not Dashboards

Every week, you receive:

1. A prioritized list of issues—ranked by expected revenue impact
2. Specific recommendations for each issue
3. The reasoning behind each recommendation
4. Expected outcome if you implement

Example recommendation:

┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ ISSUE: Mobile product page exit rate 34% higher than desktop   │
│ IMPACT: Estimated ~$12,400/month in lost revenue               │
│ RECOMMENDATION: Simplify mobile buy-box, reduce scroll depth   │
│ EXPECTED OUTCOME: 8-15% mobile CVR improvement                 │
│ PRIORITY: 1 of 7 this week                                     │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Not "your conversion rate is low."
Exactly what's wrong and what to do about it.
```

### Step 4
```
STEP 4: You Implement. The System Learns.

When you act on a recommendation:

• Mark it complete in your dashboard
• Growzilla tracks the outcome
• Future recommendations get smarter

You're not following a static playbook. You're building a system
that learns your business.

Over time, recommendations become more precise because they're
calibrated to what actually works for you.
```

```
What Makes This Different

Most analytics tools show you what happened.
Growzilla shows you what to do about it.

Analytics Tools (Triple Whale, GA4, Northbeam, etc.):
✓ Show you metrics and trends
✓ Aggregate data from multiple sources
✓ Provide attribution modeling
✗ Don't diagnose specific problems
✗ Don't prioritize what to fix
✗ Don't tell you the expected impact

Growzilla:
✓ Connects to your existing analytics tools
✓ Identifies specific friction points
✓ Ranks problems by expected revenue impact
✓ Provides specific, actionable recommendations
✓ Learns from your implementation results

Think of it this way:
Analytics tools are thermometers. They tell you the temperature.
Growzilla is a diagnostic system. It tells you what's causing
the fever and which treatment to try first.

## SECTION 7: FAQ

### Q: I already have analytics tools. Why do I need this?
```
Growzilla doesn't replace your tools—it connects them.

We pull data from Shopify, GA4, Meta, Google, Klaviyo, and others.
You keep using everything you use now. Growzilla adds an analytical
layer on top that identifies problems and prioritizes fixes.
```

### Q: How is this different from Triple Whale?
```
Triple Whale shows you metrics and dashboards.
Growzilla tells you what to do about them.

We identify specific issues, rank them by expected impact, and tell
you exactly what to change. Not more data to interpret—direction
to act on.
```

### Q: What if it doesn't work for my business?
```
That's why we have a 30-day trial with no credit card required.

During onboarding, we'll review your current setup and give you a
realistic estimate of what to expect. If the fit isn't right,
we'll tell you.
```

### Q: I don't have time to learn another tool.
```
Growzilla sends you a weekly email with ranked recommendations.
You can act on them without ever logging into a dashboard.

No training required. No certification. No learning curve.
Just open the email, see the priorities, take action.
```

### Q: What if I want to cancel?
```
Month-to-month. Cancel anytime from your dashboard.
Export your data whenever you want.
No retention tactics, no guilting, no hoops.
```

### Q: Is my data secure?
```
Yes. We're based in Sweden and GDPR-compliant by default.

Your data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We read your data
to provide analysis—we never write to your systems or share your
data with third parties.
```

### Q: How long before I see results?
```
You'll receive your first recommendations within 24-48 hours
of connecting your tools.

Operators who implement recommendations typically see measurable
improvement within 2-3 weeks. Full payback on the subscription
usually happens within the first month.
