# Growzilla ICP Definitions
> Last Updated: January 2026

## Executive Summary

Growzilla serves **experienced ecommerce operators** who have product-market fit but struggle to scale efficiently. The core tension: they've outgrown basic tools but can't afford enterprise solutions or the teams to run them. Growzilla's AI-native approach addresses this gap.

**They say:**
- "My ROAS is tanking"
- "Attribution is broken since iOS"
- "I'm spending $X on Y tools and still don't know what's working"
- "My agency says we need more budget"
- "Competitors are crushing it and I don't know how"

**They search for:**
- "Best Shopify analytics tools 2026"
- "Triple Whale alternatives"
- "How to improve conversion rate Shopify"
- "Ecommerce attribution post iOS"
- "Is [tool] worth it"

**They trust:**
- Other founders at similar scale
- Twitter/X ecommerce community
- Industry podcasts
- Case studies with specific numbers
- Shopify App Store reviews

**They fear:**
- Wasting money on another tool that doesn't work
- Complex setup/implementation
- Losing historical data
- Being locked into contracts
- Looking stupid to their team/board

## Recommended Persona Development

### Primary Persona: "Growth-Stage Greg"

**Demographics:**
- Founder/CEO or Head of Growth
- 28-42 years old
- $1M-$5M DTC brand on Shopify
- 3-8 person team

**Story:**
Greg built his brand from zero to $2M in 3 years. He's running Meta and Google ads, using Klaviyo for email, and has basic Shopify analytics. But he hit a wall. CAC is up 40% year over year, he doesn't trust his attribution, and he's drowning in dashboards. He knows AI is the future but doesn't have time to become an AI expert. He needs something that "just works" and proves ROI quickly.

**Quote:**
*"I don't need more data. I need to know what to do with the data I have."*

---

### Secondary Persona: "Enterprise Emma"

**Demographics:**
- CMO or VP Marketing
- 35-50 years old
- $10M-$50M omnichannel brand
- Reports to CEO/Board

**Story:**
Emma manages a team of 12 across marketing and customer experience. Her tech stack is complex - Shopify Plus, Salesforce, multiple ad platforms, and a patchwork of point solutions. She's under pressure to improve efficiency while maintaining growth. She's evaluated enterprise solutions but they're overkill and take 6 months to implement. She needs something powerful but pragmatic.

**Quote:**
*"I need enterprise capabilities without enterprise headaches."*

## Implications for Growzilla

### Product Priorities
1. **Shopify-native** for Segment B (non-negotiable)
2. **Quick time-to-value** (< 24 hours to first insight)
3. **Clear ROI proof** (calculators, dashboards showing savings)
4. **Easy consolidation** (import data from existing tools)
5. **Community value-add** (Elite membership as upgrade path)

### Pricing Implications
- **Entry tier:** $99-149/month (compete with Triple Whale Growth)
- **Pro tier:** $299-499/month (replace 3+ tools)
- **Enterprise:** Custom ($1,500-5,000/month for RetailOS)

### Messaging Priorities
1. Lead with consolidation: "Replace 5 tools with 1 AI"
2. Prove ROI immediately: "$47M recovered" / calculators
3. Reduce risk: Free trial, no long contracts, migration help
4. Social proof: Brands at their stage, specific metrics
