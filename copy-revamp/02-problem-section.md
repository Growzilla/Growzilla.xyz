# Problem Section Copy (Revised)

---

## Section Purpose

Establish the problem clearly and credibly. The reader should think:
- "Yes, this is exactly my situation"
- "This person understands my business"
- "This isn't hype—this is reality"

---

## Section Header

### Current (Wrong)
```
Your Store is Bleeding Revenue
7-8 figure merchants are losing $237K+/year to these leaks
```
*Problem: Hyperbolic, aggressive, sounds like fear-mongering*

### Revised (Right)
```
The Real Problem Isn't Your Data. It's Your Data Architecture.
```

**Subhead:**
```
You're paying for 5-8 different tools. Each one shows you a piece of the picture.
None of them shows you the full picture—or tells you what to do about it.
```

## Supporting Data Points (Use Sparingly)

Only include statistics that are:
1. Specific and verifiable
2. Relevant to the ICP's experience
3. Not hyperbolic or fear-inducing

**Appropriate:**
- "Average ecommerce store uses 7.3 tools for marketing and analytics" (cite source)
- "Post-iOS 14.5, attribution accuracy dropped 30-40% for most brands" (industry known)
- "The median Shopify store has a 1.8% conversion rate" (Shopify benchmark)

**Inappropriate:**
- "You're LOSING $237K/year!" (unverifiable, fear-inducing)
- "Guesswork = DEATH" (hyperbolic)
- "Crush your competitors" (aggressive)

---

## Tone Calibration

| Element | Wrong Tone | Right Tone |
|---------|------------|------------|
| Problem framing | "Your store is BLEEDING" | "Your tools don't communicate" |
| Statistics | "$237K LOST every year" | "Decisions based on incomplete data" |
| Emotional hook | Fear and FOMO | Recognition and frustration |
| Reader relationship | "You're doing it wrong" | "This is a common structural problem" |
| Solution tease | "Growzilla CRUSHES these" | "There's a structural fix for this" |

---

## What This Section Should Accomplish

By the end of this section, the reader should think:

✅ "Yes, I have this exact problem"
✅ "I've felt this frustration but couldn't articulate it"
✅ "This person understands ecommerce operations"
✅ "This sounds like a structural issue, not a personal failing"
✅ "I wonder what the solution looks like"

NOT:

❌ "This is trying to scare me into buying"
❌ "These numbers seem made up"
❌ "This is the same hype I see everywhere"
❌ "I'm being talked down to"
