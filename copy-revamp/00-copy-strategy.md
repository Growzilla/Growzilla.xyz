## The Revised Voice

### Tone Attributes

| Attribute | What It Means | What to Avoid |
|-----------|---------------|---------------|
| **Calm confidence** | We've seen this before. We know what works. | Urgency, FOMO, breathlessness |
| **Operational logic** | Clear cause → effect reasoning | Vague promises, magic thinking |
| **Slight contrarianism** | "While others chase trends, we focus on..." | Bandwagon language ("everyone's using AI") |
| **Specific and grounded** | Exact numbers, concrete mechanisms | Ranges, superlatives, abstractions |
| **Respectful of experience** | Acknowledge what they already know | Talking down, oversimplifying |

### Voice Examples

**Wrong (current):**
> "Stop guessing why customers leave. Our AI artifact analyzes your traffic data, stomps out conversion leaks, and unleashes explosive growth."

**Right (revised):**
> "Your analytics tools show you what happened. They don't show you why it happened, or what to do about it. Growzilla connects the data you already have, identifies the specific friction points causing lost sales, and generates ranked recommendations based on expected impact."

### Language Guidelines

**Use:**
- "Data shows that..." (evidence-first)
- "The specific issue is..." (precision)
- "Here's how it works..." (transparency)
- "Based on [concrete metric]..." (grounded claims)
- "You likely already know..." (respect for experience)

**Avoid:**
- "Revolutionary", "Game-changing", "Transform"
- "Unleash", "Crush", "Explode", "Monster"
- Emojis in professional copy
- "Join the herd" / community-as-FOMO
- "AI will change everything" framing
- Countdown timers, fake scarcity

## Core Positioning (Revised)

### What Growzilla Is

**One sentence:**
> Growzilla connects your existing ecommerce data, identifies the specific points where you're losing revenue, and generates prioritized recommendations you can act on.

**Expanded:**
> Most ecommerce operators have the data they need—scattered across Shopify, ad platforms, email tools, and analytics dashboards. The problem isn't data collection. It's synthesis: understanding which signals matter, where the real problems are, and what to do first.
>
> Growzilla acts as an analytical layer that sits on top of your existing stack. It connects your data sources, applies pattern recognition to identify conversion friction, and delivers specific, ranked recommendations. Not another dashboard. A diagnostic system with clear next steps.

### What Growzilla Is Not

- **Not a replacement for your existing tools.** We connect to Shopify, Klaviyo, Meta, Google—we don't replace them.
- **Not a dashboard company.** We don't show you charts and leave you to figure out what they mean.
- **Not an AI chatbot or sales agent.** That's a separate product (RetailOS) for different use cases.
- **Not enterprise software requiring a data team.** If you can use Shopify, you can use Growzilla.
- **Not a trend.** This is infrastructure—a permanent upgrade to how you operate.

### Who Growzilla Is For

Growzilla works best for:
- **Established ecommerce businesses** with $500K-$10M in annual revenue
- **Operators who already have tools** but struggle to make sense of the data
- **Decision-makers** who want clear recommendations, not more charts
- **Teams without dedicated analysts** who need leverage on their time

Growzilla is not for:
- **Brand new stores** without meaningful traffic data yet
- **Operators who want to tinker** with raw data themselves
- **Businesses needing enterprise-level compliance** (custom contracts, SLAs)
- **Anyone looking for a magic solution** that doesn't require implementation

## Message Hierarchy

Every page should answer these questions in order:

### 1. What problem exists?
Your ecommerce data is fragmented across 5-8 tools. Each shows a piece of the picture. None shows the full picture or tells you what to do.

### 2. Why does this problem exist now?
iOS privacy changes broke attribution. Ad costs rose 40% in two years. Margins compressed. The old approach—hiring agencies, adding more tools—no longer scales.

### 3. Why don't existing tools solve it?
Analytics tools (Triple Whale, GA4) show you what happened. They don't diagnose why or prescribe what to do. Attribution tools (Northbeam) require data teams to interpret. You need a system that connects the dots and tells you the next action.

### 4. How does Growzilla solve it?
Growzilla connects to your existing data sources, runs continuous analysis, identifies the specific points losing revenue, and generates ranked recommendations. You get a prioritized list of actions with expected impact—not more data to interpret.

### 5. What does using it look like?
Install in 90 seconds (one-click Shopify connection). First analysis within 24 hours. Weekly recommendations delivered via email or dashboard. Each recommendation includes what to change, why it matters, and expected impact.

### 6. What results to expect?
Customers who implement recommendations see 15-35% improvement in conversion rates. Median payback period: 3 weeks. Results depend on traffic volume and current baseline—we'll give you a realistic estimate during onboarding.

### 7. Why is switching safe?
No lock-in contracts. 30-day trial period. Works alongside your existing tools (doesn't replace them). Data export available anytime. Based in Sweden—GDPR-native from day one.

## Value Equation Framework

Every section of copy should reinforce:

```
          (Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood)
Value = ─────────────────────────────────────────
           (Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice)
```

### Increasing Numerator (Value)

- Know exactly where you're losing revenue

- Modernize operations without hiring more people

- No hype—just operational logic

- Recommendations begin immediately
- No 6-month implementation

**Effort & Sacrifice:**
- Works with existing tools (no migration)
- No technical skills required
- No training or certification
- Month-to-month, cancel anytime
