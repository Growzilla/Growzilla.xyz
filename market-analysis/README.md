## Executive Summary

### Who We Serve

**Primary ICP:** Growth-stage Shopify merchants ($500K-$10M revenue)
- Marketing-heavy teams drowning in fragmented data
- Need clarity on what's working and what to fix
- Willing to pay $149-499/month for tools that prove ROI

**Secondary ICP:** Mid-market operators ($5M-$30M revenue)
- Complex tech stacks, seeking consolidation
- Need enterprise power without enterprise complexity
- RetailOS target for AI sales agent

### Who We Compete With

| Competitor | Their Position | Our Counter |
|------------|----------------|-------------|
| Triple Whale | "Source of truth dashboard" | "We don't show data, we tell you what to do" |
| Northbeam | "Enterprise attribution" | "Enterprise power, SMB simplicity" |
| Klaviyo | "Email marketing" | "We're the brain that makes Klaviyo smarter" |
| Tidio/Intercom | "AI chatbot" | "AI that sells, not just answers" (RetailOS) |

### How Customers Find & Buy

1. **Shopify App Store** (table stakes - must be there)
2. **SEO/Content** (highest ROI, 6-12 month investment)
3. **Community** (underutilized opportunity)
4. **Partnerships** (agencies, tech integrations)
5. **Paid ads** (expensive but necessary for retargeting)

### How We Position

**Growzilla is:** The AI that finds and fixes conversion leaks

**Growzilla is not:** Another dashboard, attribution tool, or AI-washed legacy software

**Why switching feels safe:**
- 90-second install, no dev required
- Free trial / store audit
- $47M+ recovered for 200+ brands (proof)
- Works with existing tools (complement, not replace)
- Sweden-based = GDPR-native
