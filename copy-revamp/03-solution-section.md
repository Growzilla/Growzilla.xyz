# Solution & Features Section Copy (Revised)

---

## Section Purpose

Explain how Growzilla solves the problem—with clarity, not magic. The reader should understand:
- The mechanism (how it actually works)
- The output (what they'll get)
- The process (what they'll do)
- The timeline (when they'll see results)

## The Process (4 Steps)

### Step 1: Connect Your Data (Not "Install The Artifact")

**Current:**
```
ICP Archetype Detection
AI identifies your perfect customer profile automatically.
Stop wasting ad $ on wrong audiences
```
*Problem: Proprietary names mean nothing. Feature-first, not benefit-first.*

**Revised:**
```
STEP 1
Connect Your Existing Tools

One-click connection to Shopify. API connections to Meta,
Google, Klaviyo, and your other tools.

We don't replace your stack—we connect it.

• 90-second Shopify installation
• No developer required
• Your data stays in your tools
• We read, we don't write
```

**What happens:** Growzilla pulls order data, traffic data, ad performance,
and customer behavior into a unified data model.

---

### Step 2: Analysis Runs Automatically

**Current:**
```
Pareto 80/20 Optimization
Auto-surfaces the 20% of products driving 80% of revenue.
Focus where it matters most
```

**Revised:**
```
STEP 2
Continuous Analysis Begins

Within 24 hours, Growzilla has analyzed:
• Where traffic drops off in your funnel
• Which ad campaigns actually drive profitable orders
• Which products convert vs. which attract but don't sell
• Where mobile experience differs from desktop
• Which customer segments have highest LTV

This runs continuously. Not once—always.
```

**What happens:** Pattern recognition identifies the specific friction
points causing lost revenue in your unique situation.

---

### Step 3: You Get Specific Recommendations

**Current:**
```
Ad Targeting Prescriptions
Get exact audience params to feed Meta/TikTok/Google.
No more guesswork campaigns
```

**Revised:**
```
STEP 3
Ranked Recommendations, Not Dashboards

Every week, you receive:

1. A prioritized list of issues—ranked by expected revenue impact
2. Specific recommendations for each issue
3. The reasoning behind each recommendation
4. Expected outcome if you implement

Example:
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ ISSUE: Mobile product page exit rate 34% higher than desktop   │
│ IMPACT: ~$12,400/month in lost revenue                         │
│ RECOMMENDATION: Simplify mobile buy-box, reduce scroll depth   │
│ EXPECTED OUTCOME: 8-15% mobile CVR improvement                  │
│ PRIORITY: 1 of 7 this week                                      │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

Not "your conversion rate is low."
Exactly what's wrong and what to do.
```

---

### Step 4: Implementation and Feedback Loop

**Current:**
```
Store Redesign Recommendations
AI detects UX friction and conversion killers.
Get actionable fixes instantly
```

**Revised:**
```
STEP 4
You Implement. The System Learns.

When you act on a recommendation:
• Mark it complete in your dashboard
• Growzilla tracks the outcome
• Future recommendations get smarter

You're not following a static playbook.
You're building a system that learns your business.

Over time, recommendations become more precise
because they're calibrated to what works for you.
```

## Features Reframed (Problem → Solution)

Instead of listing features with proprietary names, explain what problems they solve:

| Old Feature Name | What Problem It Solves | Revised Framing |
|------------------|------------------------|-----------------|
| ICP Archetype AI | "I don't know who my best customers are" | **Customer Segmentation Analysis**: See which customer profiles have highest LTV—and where to find more of them |
| Pareto 80/20 Optimization | "I'm spread too thin across too many products" | **Product Performance Ranking**: Know which products drive profit vs. which just drive traffic |
| Ad Targeting Prescriptions | "My ad targeting is based on guesswork" | **Audience Intelligence**: Get specific audience parameters based on actual high-value customers |
| Store Redesign AI | "I don't know why people aren't buying" | **Conversion Friction Detection**: See exactly where customers drop off and why |
