## Hero Copy Rationale

| Element | Current (Wrong) | Revised (Right) | Why |
|---------|-----------------|-----------------|-----|
| Badge | "200+ brands in the herd" | "Used by 200+ ecommerce operators doing $500K-$30M annually" | Factual qualifier, not tribal identity |
| Headline | "UNLEASH GROWZILLA ON YOUR STORE" | "Your Data Isn't the Problem..." | Problem recognition, not hype |
| Subhead | "AI artifact stomps out conversion leaks" | "Connects your tools... identifies where you're losing revenue" | Clear mechanism, not metaphor |
| Value props | Emojis + vague features | Specific metrics + timelines | Operational logic |
| Primary CTA | "Install The Artifact" | "See How It Works" | Low commitment first, builds understanding |
| Secondary CTA | "Join The Herd" (Discord) | "Start 30-Day Trial" | Action-oriented, not community-first |
| Trust line | "Only 8 spots left" | "Month-to-month. Cancel anytime." | Risk reduction, not false scarcity |

## What NOT to Include in Hero

- ❌ Godzilla imagery or monster metaphors
- ❌ "Crushing", "unleashing", "explosive growth"
- ❌ Cohort dates or fake scarcity
- ❌ Emojis
- ❌ "Elite", "herd", tribal language
- ❌ Multiple competing CTAs
- ❌ Long feature lists
- ❌ Competitor callouts
