# ICP Definition & Exclusions
> Who Growzilla is for, who it's not for, and the specific offer

---

## The Offer (Explicit)

### What You Get

**Growzilla is a diagnostic analytics layer for ecommerce operators.**

When you sign up, you receive:

1. **Connection to your existing tools**
   - One-click Shopify integration
   - API connections to Meta Ads, Google Ads, Klaviyo, and other tools
   - Your data is pulled into a unified model

2. **Continuous analysis**
   - Pattern recognition runs 24/7
   - Identifies friction points in your funnel
   - Correlates ad spend with actual revenue
   - Segments customers by value

3. **Weekly ranked recommendations**
   - Prioritized list of issues by expected revenue impact
   - Specific recommendations for each issue
   - The reasoning behind each recommendation
   - Expected outcome if you implement

4. **Learning feedback loop**
   - Mark recommendations as implemented
   - System tracks outcomes
   - Future recommendations calibrate to your business

### What You Don't Get

Growzilla is **not**:

- **A replacement for your existing tools.** We connect to Shopify, Klaviyo, Meta, Google—we don't replace them. You'll still use those tools.

- **A dashboard company.** We have a dashboard, but that's not the point. The point is the recommendations. You can act on recommendations without ever logging in.

- **An AI chatbot or sales agent.** That's a separate product (RetailOS) for different use cases.

- **A managed service.** We don't implement changes for you. We tell you what to change—you (or your team) implement it.

- **Magic.** Results require you to act on recommendations. If you don't implement, you won't see improvement.

- Owner, operator, or executive of an ecommerce business
- $500K - $10M in annual revenue
- Team of 2-15 people
- On Shopify (standard or Plus)

- Business-literate but not "AI-native"
- Skeptical of hype, responsive to logic
- Feeling pressure: margin compression, rising ad costs

- Wants to modernize without risking stability

**Current Situation:**
- Using 5-8 different tools (Shopify, Klaviyo, GA4, Meta Ads, etc.)
- Spending 3-5 hours/week reconciling data across tools
- Has some analytics (maybe Triple Whale or native Shopify)
- Knows conversion could be better but unsure where to focus
- Frustrated by agencies that promise but don't deliver

**What They're Looking For:**
- Clarity on what's actually working and what isn't
- Specific direction, not more data to interpret
- A system that compounds—gets smarter over time
- Low-risk way to modernize operations
- Something they can evaluate rationally, not emotionally

**Demographics:**

- Head of Growth, Marketing Director, or similar
- Reports to founder/CEO
- $2M - $20M company revenue

**Psychographics:**
- Sophisticated marketeer, data-comfortable

- Under pressure to prove ROI on marketing spend

**What They're Looking For:**

## Who Growzilla Is NOT For

### Explicitly Not a Fit

1. **Brand new stores with no traffic**
   - You need data for analysis. If you're doing <$10K/month, there's not enough signal to analyze.
   - Come back when you have 3-6 months of consistent traffic.

2. **Operators who want to DIY everything**
   - If you prefer building your own dashboards in Sheets or Looker, Growzilla isn't for you.
   - We're for operators who want recommendations, not raw data.

3. **Enterprise with complex compliance needs**
   - If you need SOC2, custom contracts, or dedicated SLAs, we're not there yet.
   - RetailOS Enterprise handles these cases, but core Growzilla is self-serve.

4. **Anyone looking for magic**
   - Growzilla finds problems and recommends fixes. You still have to implement.
   - If you're looking for "set it and forget it," this isn't it.

5. **Platforms other than Shopify (for now)**
   - Core Growzilla requires Shopify.
   - WooCommerce, BigCommerce, Magento users: join the waitlist, but we're not there yet.

6. **Businesses that don't use paid ads**
   - A significant part of our value comes from connecting ad data to revenue.
   - If you're 100% organic/SEO, you'll get less value.

### Warning Signs (May Not Be a Fit)

- "I need this to run my whole marketing"
- "I don't have time to implement anything"
- "I want someone to manage this for me"
- "I'm launching next month and need everything ready"
- "Can you guarantee results?"
