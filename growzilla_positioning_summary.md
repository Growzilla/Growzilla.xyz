# Growzilla Positioning Summary

## Core Direction

-   Be specific. No jargon.
-   Target: Shopify merchants / Shopify brands.
-   Lead with outcome: increase conversion.
-   Secondary value: build scalable systems that support growth.
-   Avoid abstract language like "applied AI systems for operators."
-   Clarity \> sophistication.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Hero Positioning (Clear & Direct)

Recommended direction:

> We Increase Shopify Conversion and Build Scalable Systems.

Alternative: \> We Help Shopify Merchants Increase Conversion and Build
Systems That Drive Growth.

Short. Concrete. Outcome-focused.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## What We Actually Are

-   Not a big growth consultancy.
-   Not a generic AI agency.
-   A small, sharp systems-focused team.
-   We scope tightly.
-   We extract signal fast.
-   We build structured interventions that create leverage.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Messaging Principles

-   No inflated claims.
-   No "AI transformation" language.
-   No copying 100M agency voice.
-   Sound precise, not big.
-   Use real team, real builds, real demos.
-   Replace fluff with specificity.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Site Structure (Lean & High Signal)

1.  Clear Hero (Shopify + Conversion + Systems)
2.  Clear Problem (Conversion leaks, structural gaps)
3.  What We Do (Analyze → Identify → Scope → Build)
4.  Proof (Real demo, real tool, real names)
5.  Team (Actual people, headshots, short bios)
6.  CTA (Conversion Review / Systems Review)

Remove: - Generic testimonials - Abstract positioning - Vague growth
language

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Proof Density

To reduce due diligence friction:

-   Real demo link
-   Real case (even small, but honest)
-   Real team headshots
-   Real advisor name
-   References "available on request"
-   State that additional case material is being compiled

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Tone

-   Founder memo energy.
-   Calm confidence.
-   Specific over broad.
-   Early but serious.
-   No apology for stage.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Design Notes

-   Big logo scroll section (credibility anchor).
-   Keep visuals strong, copy tight.
-   Replace filler sections with proof.
-   Make it feel owned, not AI-generated.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

## Internal Reminder

We are early --- that's fine. What matters: - Clear positioning. - Clean
execution. - Real proof. - Tight scope.

Clarity reduces friction. Specificity increases trust.
