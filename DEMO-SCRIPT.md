# Demo Recording Scripts — Creator Revenue Hub

## Setup Before Recording
1. Open `growzilla.xyz/smdashboard` in Chrome
2. Set browser to 1920x1080 (full HD)
3. Clear any overlays/extensions
4. Use OBS or screen recorder at 60fps
5. Practice the click sequence once before recording

## 30-Second Demo Script (Instagram Reel / TikTok)

### Hook (0-3 seconds)
**On screen**: Dashboard loads with org view, KPI cards animate in
**Say**: "What if you could see exactly how much money each creator post makes you?"

### Body (3-20 seconds)
**Action**: Point at Total Revenue ($284.7K), then Creator Earnings ($42.7K)
**Say**: "Growzilla tracks every creator, every post, every platform — and shows you the exact revenue."

**Action**: Click on "Sarah Chen" in Creator Performance section
**Say**: "Click any creator to see their posts with real revenue numbers."

**Action**: Click on top post card (the $4,230 TikTok Reel)
**Say**: "Click a post — see the full conversion funnel. Views to clicks to purchases."

### CTA (20-30 seconds)
**On screen**: Post modal showing $4,230 revenue, conversion funnel, commission
**Say**: "This Reel made $4,230 in revenue. Stop guessing which content works. Link in bio."

### Technical Notes
- Keep mouse movements smooth and deliberate
- Pause 1 second on each KPI card for readability
- The post modal animation is the "wow moment" — let it breathe

## Recording Tips

1. **Speed**: Move at 70% of your natural speed. What feels slow to you looks perfect on camera.
2. **Mouse**: Use a large cursor or highlight tool. Small cursors get lost in recordings.
3. **Clicks**: Pause briefly before and after each click. Let animations complete.
4. **Voice**: Record voiceover separately for better audio quality. Sync in post.
5. **Lighting**: If showing face-cam overlay, use ring light + dark background (matches dashboard).
6. **Format**: Record at 1080p minimum. Crop to 9:16 for Reels/TikTok, 16:9 for YouTube.
7. **Captions**: Add text overlays for key numbers ($284.7K, 4.2x, etc.) in post-production.
8. **Music**: Use trending audio on TikTok/Reels. Muted background music for YouTube.
